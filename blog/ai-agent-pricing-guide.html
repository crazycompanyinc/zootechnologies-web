Why Maintenance Eats Your Budget

AI agents aren't "ship and forget." They need: prompt tuning as models update, monitoring for drift, security patches for new vulnerabilities, integration updates when APIs change, and scaling adjustments as usage grows. Budget 15-20 hours/month minimum per agent.

When Building From Scratch Makes Sense

We're not anti-build. There are legitimate reasons to build custom:

  • Core IP: The agent IS your product (not just internal tooling)
  • Unique requirements: No existing solution handles your specific use case
  • Regulatory needs: You need full control over data flow and model choices
  • Scale: You're operating at a scale where per-unit economics favor custom

But be honest: 80% of "we need custom" cases are actually "we haven't evaluated pre-built options carefully enough."

The Hybrid Approach (What Smart Startups Do)

The fastest path for most startups:

  1. Start with pre-built — Ship your first agent in days, not months
  2. Validate the use case — Confirm agents actually save time/money for YOUR workflow
  3. Customize incrementally — Replace pre-built components with custom code only where it matters
  4. Scale what works — Double down on agents that prove ROI

This approach gets you to market 3–6 months faster and costs 90%+ less than full custom build.
